Salil Parekh, the CEO of Infosys
born on August 19, 1964. 6 min read

Introduction Salil Parekh, the CEO of Infosys, stands as a pivotal figure in the global IT services industry. Since taking the helm of Infosys in January 2018, Parekh has guided the company through a period of significant transformation and growth. His leadership is characterized by a focus on digital innovation, client-centric strategies, and operational excellence, positioning Infosys as a key player in the evolving tech landscape. Early Life and Education Salil Parekh was born on August 19, 1964, in India. He pursued his engineering degree in Aeronautics from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay, one of the premier engineering institutions in India. After completing his undergraduate studies, Parekh went on to earn a Master’s degree in Computer Science from Cornell University, equipping him with a strong foundation in both technology and management. Career Before Infosys Prior to joining Infosys, Salil Parekh had a distinguished career at Capgemini, a global consulting and technology services firm. At Capgemini, Parekh served as a member of the Group Executive Board and was responsible for the company’s North American business and global services. His tenure at Capgemini was marked by successful growth strategies and a focus on enhancing client relationships and operational efficiency. Before Capgemini, Parekh worked at various firms, including the French multinational consulting company, where he gained extensive experience in leading large-scale technology projects and managing complex client engagements. His background in both technology and consulting provided him with a broad perspective on the IT services industry, which he brought with him to Infosys. Leadership at Infosys Salil Parekh's appointment as CEO of Infosys marked a new chapter for the company, which had been navigating through a period of significant change and leadership transitions. Parekh’s leadership has been characterized by a strategic emphasis on digital transformation, operational efficiency, and client-centricity. One of Parekh’s key priorities has been driving Infosys’s shift towards digital services, including cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and data analytics. Under his leadership, Infosys has made substantial investments in these areas, aiming to provide innovative solutions that help clients navigate the complexities of digital transformation. Parekh has also focused on strengthening Infosys’s position in key markets and expanding the company’s global footprint. Strategic Initiatives and Growth Salil Parekh has implemented several strategic initiatives to drive Infosys’s growth and enhance its competitive edge. His focus on digital transformation has involved expanding the company’s capabilities in areas such as cloud services, artificial intelligence, and automation. Infosys has launched various new offerings and partnerships to support clients in their digital journeys, positioning itself as a leader in next-generation technologies. Under Parekh’s leadership, Infosys has also placed a strong emphasis on enhancing client relationships and delivering value through customized solutions. The company has adopted a client-centric approach, working closely with customers to understand their unique needs and develop tailored strategies that address their business challenges. Another significant aspect of Parekh’s strategy has been improving operational efficiency and agility. Infosys has invested in streamlining its internal processes, leveraging technology to drive efficiency, and fostering a culture of innovation and continuous improvement. These efforts have helped the company respond more effectively to market changes and client demands. Challenges and Achievements Salil Parekh’s tenure at Infosys has not been without challenges. The global IT services industry is highly competitive, with rapid technological advancements and shifting client expectations. Parekh has navigated these challenges by focusing on innovation, operational excellence, and strategic growth initiatives. One of the notable achievements during Parekh’s leadership has been Infosys’s consistent financial performance and growth. The company has reported strong revenue and profit growth, driven by its focus on digital services and strategic investments. Parekh’s leadership has also been recognized for fostering a positive organizational culture and enhancing Infosys’s reputation as a leading global IT services provider. Future Outlook As Infosys continues to evolve under Salil Parekh’s leadership, the company is poised to play a significant role in shaping the future of the IT services industry. Parekh’s vision for Infosys includes further advancements in digital technologies, expansion into new markets, and continued investment in innovation. His leadership will be crucial in navigating the ever-changing technology landscape and driving the company’s growth and success in the years to come. Conclusion Salil Parekh’s tenure as CEO of Infosys has been marked by strategic vision, digital innovation, and a client-centric approach. His leadership has positioned Infosys as a key player in the global IT services industry, driving growth and transformation in an era of rapid technological change. As Infosys continues to adapt and thrive under Parekh’s guidance, his impact on the company and the broader technology sector will undoubtedly be significant.
